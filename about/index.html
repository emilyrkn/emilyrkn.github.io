about me


As an undergraduate at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, I am interested in phonetics and speech perception. I am a research assistant in the Phonetics Lab.

My curiosities are currently centered around the general auditory processes of perception, the relationship between perception and production, the phonetics-phonology interface, and voicing contrasts in languages around the world.

After finishing my undergraduate studies, I hope to pursue graduate school to continue my exploration of speech perception.

Outside of linguistics, I am an avid film-watcher and consumer of pad thai.
